FOOT

Stand with feet shoulder width apart. Bend your knees and do a deep squat, resting your hands on the floor. Jump back and take the plank position. The body should maintain a straight line, the hips and lower back should not bend to the floor. The palms are strictly under the shoulder joints. Bend your elbows back and touch the floor with your chest, while your body remains a straight line. Return to the plank position, keeping a straight line of the body. Jump forward by pulling your knees to your chest. Hips parallel to the floor, do not lift your buttocks up. Jump out sharply, raising your arms and body up.

X10/3 repeat
