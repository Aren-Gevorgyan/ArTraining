An individually selected set of simple exercises for thin legs or for weight loss that do not require additional equipment will allow you to quickly pump up your legs or lose weight at home.Technically, the exercise is simple, it allows you to increase the explosive power of the femoral muscles, develops coordination and prepares for increasing loads. Jumping out of the squat is a traumatic movement, the back and knees can suffer, therefore it is performed with minimal additional load (dumbbells) or with its own weight. If a person’s weight is much higher than normal, then this type of exercise is contraindicated for him.
