FOOT

Starting position: feet shoulder width apart. Look strictly in front of you. Slowly, as you inhale, begin to crouch so that your knees do not go forward stop, and at the lower point the rear surface of the trouble was parallel to the floor. Be careful not to round your back. From the bottom point, on the exhale, return to the starting position. Squats - the best exercise for the development of the front surface of the thigh, as well as the gluteus maximus muscle.To pump up your legs at home, you can adapt plastic containers of various sizes filled with sand or water as extra weight.

X15/3 repeat

FOOT

Lunges with legs forward, backward and to the side perfectly work out all the muscles of the legs. When performing the step, make it as wide as possible. The heel of the leg with lunges forward or back should be in line with the knee of the other leg bent to the floor. To return to the starting position, straighten the leg in front with force.For weighting when doing lunges, it is more convenient to use dumbbells. They are held in lowered hands.

X10/3 repeat
