PRESS

  • Lie on the floor, stretch your legs, arms along the body.
  • Raise your legs and shoulders low from the floor - this is the starting position.
  • Lift the body with a straight back and legs bent at the knees, only the pelvis remains on the floor.
  • Return to starting position and repeat 25 times.
